About

My first venture was graduating from Felix Varela High School and experiencing the world on my own. Worked on many jobs from hard labor to supervisor and It was at that time that taught me about never settling and to always improve myself. But no matter the job my passion for computers has always stayed with me. From there, I decided to pursue an education in computer coding at the University of Miami Boot Camp. I learned the skills necessary at the Academy, a web development boot camp, to become a Full-Stack Developer. Since then my toolbox consists of languages such as Swift, Python, Ruby, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and JQuery; frameworks such as Angular.js, Express.js, Node.js, Rails, Sinatra, Angular, Bootstrap, and Materialize; databases such as MySQL and MongoDB; and technologies such as Heroku, and Firebase.

I overcome the challenges that come with boot camps. My learning does not stop there. I am currently seeking an AWS Certification and a Degree in Computer Science, learning other languages, and I am always seeking to find innovative ways to be a more efficient and productive developer.
